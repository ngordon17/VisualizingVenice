March 18, 1488
300 ducats of bronze were purchased for the casting of the statue.
Archivio di Stato di Venezia, Proveddeditori al Sal, Notatorio, 2 (1462-1493), fol. 171v.

June 25, 1488
In his last will and testament, Verrochio asks the doge to allow Lorenzo di Credi to complete the statue.
Biblioteca Riccardiana, Manuscript 2713.

October 7, 1488
Lorenzo di Credi attempts to contract for the completion of monument.
Archivio di Stato di Firenze, Notarile anti-cosimiano, 20614, Ser Antonio Ubaldini da Firenze, 1486-1493, fol. 33r-v.
