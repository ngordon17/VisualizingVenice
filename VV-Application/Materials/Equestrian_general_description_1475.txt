October 31, 1475
Bartolomeo Colleoni authorizes the final version of his will, which includes the gift of 100,000 ducats to Venice, with the proviso that an equestrian monument be erected in honor of him in Piazza San Marco.

Archivio di Stato di Bresco, Archivio Martinengo Colleoni, Testamento di Bartolomeo Colleoni, fol 26r.

November 2, 1475
Colleoni dies in Malpaga.
