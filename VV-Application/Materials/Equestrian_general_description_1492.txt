August 11, 1492
In a letter to the Duke of Milan, orator Taddeo da Vimercate writes that he had gone with the Signoria  to see the almost-completed statue.  At that point, the final location of the statue had not been determined.

Archivio di Stato di Milano, Archivio Ducale Sforzesco, Potenze Estere, Venezia, fol. 379.
